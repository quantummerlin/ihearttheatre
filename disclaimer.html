Our Review Philosophy

We Are Reviewers, NOT Critics

We are committed to celebrating theatre in all its forms. Our reviews aim to show the positives of every show reviewed, recognizing that it is possible to not personally enjoy a performance while still appreciating the art, the effort, the production, and the dedication involved.

What Our Reviews Represent

  • Personal Opinions: All reviews represent the personal views and experiences of our reviewers (Deanna Amato and Penelope Quinn) based on specific performances attended
  • Subjective Experiences: Theatre is subjective; individual experiences may vary between different performances, audiences, and viewing circumstances
  • Positive Focus: We focus on highlighting strengths, achievements, and positive aspects of productions
  • Community Spirit: Our reviews are written from a place of love and appreciation for Melbourne's theatre community

What Our Reviews Do NOT Represent

  • Professional Criticism: We are not professional theatre critics and do not claim to provide comprehensive critical analysis
  • Definitive Judgments: Our reviews do not represent the only or definitive opinion on any production
  • Expert Assessments: Reviews are not intended as expert assessments of technical or artistic merit
  • Recommendations for All: What one person enjoys may not suit another's tastes or preferences

Accuracy and Timeliness

Productions may change cast, creative teams, schedules, or other details after our review is published. Our reviews reflect the specific performance attended, which may differ from subsequent performances.
