Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we protect your information.

Introduction

At iHeartTheatre.com, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

By using iHeartTheatre.com, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use our website.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Review Submissions: Name, email address, review content, and show details when submitting a review
  • Show Submissions: Company name, contact information, show details, and promotional materials when submitting a show
  • Newsletter Signups: Email address for newsletter subscriptions
  • Contact Inquiries: Name, email address, and message when contacting us
  • Photos and Images: Production photos when submitting reviews or shows

Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Browser Information: Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Device Information: Device type and screen resolution
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, and click patterns
  • IP Address: Your IP address (anonymized where possible)

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve website performance and functionality
  • Analyze website usage patterns
  • Provide personalized content

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Services: Process and publish reviews, show listings, and submissions
  • Communicate with You: Respond to inquiries, send newsletters, and provide updates
  • Improve Our Platform: Enhance user experience, develop new features, and fix issues
  • Analyze Usage: Understand how visitors use our website to improve content and functionality
  • Ensure Security: Prevent fraud and protect against unauthorized access
  • Comply with Legal Obligations: Meet applicable laws and regulations

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Public Content: Reviews and show submissions may be published publicly on our website with the information you provide (name, show details, review content, etc.)
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our website (e.g., hosting, analytics, email services) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice)
  • With Your Consent: When you have given us explicit consent to share your information

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Security Measures We Use

  • Secure HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure storage of user data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication procedures
  • Monitoring for security breaches

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

  • Reviews: Published reviews are retained indefinitely unless requested for removal
  • Contact Information: Contact details are retained for communication purposes
  • Newsletter Subscribers: Email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe
  • Website Analytics: Usage data is retained for analysis and improvement purposes

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Request transfer of your information to another service
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your information
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for processing where consent is the legal basis

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our website includes content and features specifically designed for children and young people, such as Penelope's Junior Reviews. We are particularly careful when handling information from children under 13 years of age.

Our Commitment to Young Users

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • Young reviewers' submissions are reviewed and supervised by parents/guardians
  • Personal information about minors is limited and protected
  • Parents can request access, correction, or deletion of their child's information

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, including theatre venues, ticketing platforms, and other resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification to registered users (for significant changes)

Your continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Get in Touch

We will respond to your privacy inquiries within 30 days.
