About Deanna

Deanna Amato celebrates the magic of Melbourne's vibrant theatre scene. With over 15 published reviews across diverse productions—from major professional shows to intimate community theatre performances—Deanna's love for live performance buzzes through every word, helping audiences discover shows worth experiencing and supporting performers to reach their absolute potential.


Her approach balances respect for the creative process with honest, constructive feedback. Whether reviewing a Broadway blockbuster or an emerging playwright's debut, Deanna focuses on the elements that make theatre magical: storytelling, performance, production quality, and emotional resonance.
