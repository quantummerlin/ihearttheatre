Meet Penelope

Penelope Quinn is Melbourne's youngest performance champion at just 7 years old! With a genuine love for theatre and an honest kid's perspective, Penelope celebrates the wonder of family theatre, capturing the magic that makes kids fall in love with live performance.


Her reviews focus on family-friendly and junior theatre productions, helping families discover shows that kids will truly love. Whether it's a school production, a junior musical, or a major family show, Penelope tells it like it is—from a kid's point of view!


Parents love her reviews because they help them make informed decisions about what shows are appropriate and enjoyable for children. Her enthusiasm is contagious, and her insights are refreshingly honest and age-appropriate.
